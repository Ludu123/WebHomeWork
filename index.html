Brief Introduction

Marvel Comics is an American comic book company.

Marvel was founded in 1939 and officially named Marvel in 1961 Marvel was founded by publisher Martin Goodman, who had previously focused on creating a popular magazine that covered everything from Westerns to detective stories to adventure and science fiction. By 1938, he had decided to look in a new direction -- novelty, pageantry, and gripping intensity -- and comics were just that.

Since 2008, Marvel has brought Iron Man, Hulk, Black Widow, Thor, Hawkeye and Captain America to the big screen, and then brought them all together with The Avengers -- in its first phase, Marvel has made a whopping $3.74 billion at the global box office with a combined cost of $1 billion for six films.
